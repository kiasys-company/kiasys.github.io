Who is KiaSys for

KiaSys is developed as a tool for teams responsible for coating quality and process control in industrial settings.

Production departments

Support for process engineers in selecting and monitoring process regimes, analyzing process stability and exploring “what-if” scenarios when parameters change.

Laboratories & R&D

Automated processing of microstructure images, unified experiment database and correlation of coating structure with performance properties.

Engineering companies

Integrating KiaSys into comprehensive solutions for digital twins, smart shops and condition monitoring systems.

Key system features

Coating image analysis

  • segmentation of the coating area and interface boundary;
  • evaluation of porosity, layer thicknesses and bonding defects;
  • detection of local defects (cracks, incomplete bonding, inclusions);
  • generation of defect maps and summary metrics.

Digital models & decision support

  • regression models and metamodels linking process parameters and coating structure;
  • multi-criteria optimization (TOPSIS and other criteria);
  • scenario analysis of “quality ↔ process parameters” trade-offs;
  • generation of recommendations for process parameter adjustments.
